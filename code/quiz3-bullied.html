Am I Being Bullied?

Are you worried that you or a loved one may be being bullied at work, school, or home? Take this short quiz to identify the signs of bullying and recognize if you are experiencing them.
Below is a list of questions that relate to life experiences common among people who have been bullied. Please read each question carefully, and indicate how often you have experienced the same or similar challenges in the past few months.


1. Do others make hurtful comments about you?






2. Do others imitate you or make fun of your appearance?






3. Do you feel isolated at work, school, home, or other places?






4. Are others spreading rumors or false information about you?






5. Are you ever afraid to go to work, school, or places where you feel hurt or excluded?






6. Do others physically hurt you or damage your possessions?






7. Are you being harassed online or do others post mean things about you?






8. Do you feel anxious or depressed when you have to interact with a hurtful person?






9. Do you experience headaches or stomach aches before you have to interact with a hurtful person?
