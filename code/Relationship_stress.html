Relationship Stress

Symptoms

Some common physical symptoms of stress include headaches, upset stomach, chest pains, sleep disturbances, teeth-grinding, and a general sense of low energy. If you notice yourself experiencing these symptoms frequently in relation to a partner, there's probably something wrong that needs fixing.

  • Recognize stress symptoms.
  • Approach your partner.
  • Listen.
  • Comfort first.
  • Get active together.
  • Create a list of stress-reducing rituals.
  • Check your stress temperature.
  • Ask your partner what you can do.
  • Keep posted on your partner’s days.
  • Consider if there’s anything else you can do.
